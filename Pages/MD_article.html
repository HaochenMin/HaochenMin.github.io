Mathemagician's Duel

A digital adaptation of BSGames' Mathemagician's Duel, made in Unity using C#. Features a tutorial to teach players the rules and how to play the game, a Hotseat mode where both players take turns playing on one device, and multiplayer over IP. A few custom rules had to implemented such as a timer to prevent players from stalling and prevent the game from progressing by not taking their turn.
